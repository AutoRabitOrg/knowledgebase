# Remove Plugin

### Remove Plugin

1. Go to **`Help`** -> **`Install New Software`**
   1.  Click On **`already installed`**. <br>

       <figure><img src="../../../../../../.gitbook/assets/studio-remove-plugin-already-installed.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
   2.  Select **`IZ Scan Analysis`** <br>

       <figure><img src="../../../../../../.gitbook/assets/studio-remove-plugin-installed-plugin-list.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
   3.  Click on **`Unistall`** and follow the uninstallation instructions <br>

       <figure><img src="../../../../../../.gitbook/assets/studio_remove_plugin_final.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
2. Restart Studio after uninstallation
